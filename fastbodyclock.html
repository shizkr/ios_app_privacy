FastBodyClock Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Data Collection

FastBodyClock stores your fasting history and preferences locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

Advertisements

This app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device's Settings > Privacy > Advertising.

Data Storage

All fasting records, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob: Used for displaying advertisements. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

You can delete all your data at any time by deleting the app from your device. No account creation is required to use this app.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at fastbodyclock@klife.ai.
